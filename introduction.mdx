# Welcome to Arda

Arda is a dark gothic AMOLED theme for [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/). Built around a medieval aesthetic, it combines warm parchment tones, burgundy accents, and manuscript-style decorative elements to create a unique writing experience.

## What Makes Arda Special

**Typography & Details**: Arda uses serif fonts reminiscent of old manuscripts, with small-caps headings and decorative diamond markers for lists. Every element has been carefully styled to maintain the medieval theme.

**Ornamental Styling**: From corner flourishes to sword-shaped checkboxes, Arda adds subtle decorative touches that enhance the aesthetic without overwhelming your content.

**Enhanced Components**:
- **Syntax highlighting** styled after parchment ledgers
- **Callouts** redesigned as warm note cards with subtle gradients
- **Images** with decorative framing and 3D hover effects
- **Tables** featuring stone-etched designs

## About This Theme

Arda was created by [RepRep666](https://github.com/RepRep666) as a personal project and shared with the Obsidian community. It's designed for those who appreciate a darker, more atmospheric writing environment with a touch of medieval charm.

<Note>
Arda is a dark mode only theme. Make sure your Obsidian is set to dark mode for the best experience.
</Note>
